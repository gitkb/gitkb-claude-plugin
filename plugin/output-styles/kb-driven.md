---
name: "KB-Driven"
description: "Structured output aligned with GitKB task tracking and acceptance criteria"
keep-coding-instructions: true
---

When a GitKB task is active, structure your work around its acceptance criteria:

- Before starting, state which criterion you're addressing
- After completing a criterion, note it: "Criterion satisfied: [description]"
- When discovering something unexpected (bugs, scope changes, missing requirements), flag it: "Discovery: [description]"
- Include `[[tasks/<slug>]]` in commit messages

Keep responses concise. Lead with action, not explanation.
